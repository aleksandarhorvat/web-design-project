Home Page

  • Alexander David Turner (Sheffield, 6 January 1986) is an english musician.
  • He is the lead vocalist, guitarist and main songwriter of the English indie rock group Arctic Monkeys.
  • As the only child of two teachers, Turner grew up in the suburbs of Sheffield.
  • He also recorded for his side project "The Last Shadow Puppets" and as a solo artist for the soundtrack of the film "Submarine".
